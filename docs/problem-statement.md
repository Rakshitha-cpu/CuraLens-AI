# Problem Statement

## Background

Millions of patients in rural India depend on Government Hospitals and Primary Health Centres (PHCs) for medical treatment. Doctors often write prescriptions by hand due to limited digital infrastructure.

Many patients face difficulties understanding these prescriptions because of:

- Illegible handwriting
- Medical terminology
- Language barriers
- Limited pharmacist availability
- Low health literacy

As a result, patients may:

- Take incorrect medicines
- Miss doses
- Take medicines at the wrong time
- Stop treatment early
- Experience avoidable health complications

---

## Existing Challenges

### Patients

- Cannot read handwritten prescriptions
- Forget dosage instructions
- Don't understand medicine purpose

### Healthcare Workers

- Spend time explaining prescriptions repeatedly
- Limited manpower in rural areas

### Healthcare System

- Poor medication adherence
- Increased follow-up visits
- Avoidable complications

---

## Proposed Solution

CuraLens AI is an AI-powered multi-agent medication guidance system that transforms handwritten prescriptions into understandable, multilingual, and actionable medication plans.

Using Google's AI ecosystem, CuraLens AI extracts prescription details, verifies uncertain information, explains medicines in simple language, generates medication schedules, and supports patients throughout their treatment journey.

---

## Target Users

- Rural Patients
- Government Hospitals
- Primary Health Centres (PHCs)
- Community Health Centres
- ASHA Workers
- Healthcare Volunteers

---

## Expected Impact

- Reduce medication errors
- Improve medication adherence
- Enhance patient understanding
- Support multilingual healthcare
- Improve rural healthcare accessibility
